News

CoDriver Piloted In-Retailer After more than a decade of deployment in automotive launch & training events in the UK and Europe, CoDriver is now customer-facing. The system is being piloted by a premium UK manufacturer to facilitate their customers completing fully unaccompanied test drives. CoDriver is Ignition’s bespoke in-car navigation app that delivers directional instructions as well as key narratives or ‘product stories’. These play out to the vehicle occupants at specific locations along a route or on a test drive – meaning every occupant hears the desired message at the most relevant point of their journey.
