FROM IDEAS TO REVENUES

SUPERNOVA HUB, FOUNDED BY FEDERICO POZZI CHIESA, IS THE CORPORATE VENTURE ACCELERATOR OF GC HOLDING, THE GROUP THAT CONTROLS ITALMONDO S.P.A., A WORLD LEADER IN ITALIAN AND INTERNATIONAL LOGISTICS SERVICES.

About Us

With the experience of 60 years in business, Supernova Hub offers expertise acquired in logistics and e-commerce, supporting innovative business projects through an exclusive acceleration program. Because of its expertise in IT, its network of investors and business angels, and its experience with a proven investment model. Supernova Hub utilizes a co-working space where teams from all the startups meet synergistically, forming a group of integrated companies that can create synergies while maintaining time-to-market and high performance.

THE OBJECTIVE OF SUPERNOVA HUB IS TO ACCELERATE THE GROWTH OF INNOVATIVE STARTUPS IN THE IN THE E-COMMERCE AND SMART LOGISTICS ECOSYSTEM, EXPLOITING THE TEAM’S MANY YEARS OF EXPERIENCE: WE INVEST IN PROJECTS WITH INNOVATIVE TECHNOLOGICAL SOLUTIONS IN BUSINESS SECTORS WITH STRONG GROWTH PROSPECTS AND HIGH POTENTIAL FOR SCALABILITY.

Team

FEDERICO POZZI CHIESA

CEO of GC Holding

He joined the family company as the age of 20 and now holds the position of CEO of GC Holding, the group that controls Italmondo S.p.A., a world leader in international logistics services.

From the experience of one who wrote logistics history, conceived of and founded Sendabox, the first shipment provider in Italy, an integrated logistics platform for e-commerce that completes its range of logistics services with the acquisition of the Io Ritiro platform. Sendabox was the first nucleus of Supernova Hub, the corporate venture accelerator conceived of and founded by Federico.

He is co-founder and member of the board of Termostore and Zoro.

Firmly believing in the force of innovation, he invested early on in Borsadelcredito.it, where he is now a member of the board of directors.

ALESSANDRO ANDREOZZI

General Manager of Supernova

General Manager of Supernova, co-founder and CEO of Termostore, founder of Borsa del Credito. Born at Fondi (LT), Milanese by adoption, his skills in credit processes and information technology led him to found BorsadelCredito.it together with Ivan Pellegrini.

PAOLO GIANNOTTI

General Manager of Sendabox

General Manager of Sendabox, founding investor of Borsa del Credito. In his past role as senior manager at the service of the principal Italian and international banks with Business Integrated Partners, one of Italy's principal management consulting firms, he now successfully manages the group's first startup through the experience he gained in managing international projects in digital and corporate banking with a strong technological component.

He possesses both great expertise in the IT field and a thorough knowledge of the e-commerce and logistics and shipping world. He graduated cum laude in management engineering from Università degli Studi of Bergamo.

SILVIA NUTINI

Marketing manager of Supernova.

Long experience in the development and management of complex communication and marketing projects, in Italy and abroad, for a leading international fashion and luxury company, for which she followed the startup phase in Europe.

The integration of the traditional way of communicating in the fashion world and the digital transformation affecting all sectors has become her daily 'battlefield', soliciting and supporting the new entrepreneurs in the creation of their businesses. After earning a business degree from Bocconi, she worked as a tax consultant with a well-known firm in Milan, before totally leaving that profession for marketing.
